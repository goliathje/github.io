Verhaal:
Kristin, a single 40-year-old secretary, plans to celebrate her birthday with her boss. However, her plans for a romantic evening under the candlelight will change, as Gerti, her strong-minded mother, and her partner in crime, Lenny--Kristin's best friend--have, too, plans for a surprise party. Soon, Kristin will find out that inside Lenny's present--a reclaimed suitcase from the airport's lost-and-found auction--lies a collection of love letters and personal poems of affection, addressed to a mysterious woman. Who might she be, and who would be able to write such emotional and romantic letters? Touched and inspired by the fiery content of the suitcase, Kristin will attempt to solve the mystery, and experience, for once in her life, a profound love with her one true soulmate. But, where could he be?
