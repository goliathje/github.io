Created for Hothouse, the National Film Board of Canada's emerging filmmaker apprenticeship program in 2011, 'Black Gold' was Canadian artist Aimee van Drimmelen's first introduction to hand drawn animation. Featuring her signature organic ink drawings and time lapse footage of ink moving on the surface of a drum skin.
